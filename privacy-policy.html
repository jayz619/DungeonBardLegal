Privacy Policy for Dungeon Bard

Effective Date: 27th May, 2025

Dungeon Bard (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to safeguarding your data and giving you control over your information, while also protecting our rights to evolve the app’s functionality. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and manage data when you use our mobile app.

1. Age Restriction

Dungeon Bard is strictly intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect or store any data from individuals under 18. Use of the app confirms you are of adult status and legally permitted to use it.

2. Information We Collect

  • Google Account Info (name, email, profile picture, user ID)
  • Generated content (text inputs, audio files, and metadata)
  • App usage logs (search activity, crashes, interaction stats)
  • Device data (model, OS, IP address)

3. How We Use It

  • To generate and save voice content
  • To store your library using Firebase
  • To authenticate via Google
  • To analyze and improve features

4. Data Sharing

We use Firebase for secure storage and ElevenLabs for voice generation (see their privacy policy). We do not sell personal data. We may share data when legally required.

5. Your Rights

You can delete saved audio files anytime or contact us to delete your account data. Uninstalling the app halts future data collection.

6. Updates

This policy may be updated as the app evolves. Continued use implies acceptance of the latest version.
