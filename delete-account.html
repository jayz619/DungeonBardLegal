Delete Your Account

Effective Date: 27th May, 2025

If you would like to delete your Dungeon Bard account and all associated data, you can do so in one of two ways:

  1. In-App: Open the Dungeon Bard app, go to the settings menu, and tap the "Delete Account" option.
  2. By Email: You can also request account deletion by contacting us at jackliton770@gmail.com. Please include your registered email address used with Google Sign-In for identity verification.

All account data, including any saved audio or content, will be permanently deleted within 7 days of your request.
